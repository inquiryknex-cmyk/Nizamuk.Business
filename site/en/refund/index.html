Refund Policy

NizamOk products are digital and delivered immediately after purchase by link or email, so they are treated as instantly accessible digital goods.

When can you ask for help?

  • If the product did not arrive after payment.
  • If a wrong link arrived, or a file that does not match the pattern you purchased.
  • If a technical problem prevented you from accessing the file.

Refunds

Because of the nature of digital products, a refund cannot be guaranteed after the product has been opened or downloaded, except in cases of a clear technical error or incorrect delivery. We review every case fairly and transparently.

Support

For help, write to support@nizamok.com with your purchase email and the product name.
