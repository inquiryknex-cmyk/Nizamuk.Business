The Four Patterns

Four doors… and one dominant pattern leading you

You are not a single box. The quiz reads your blend, then reveals the pattern steering your day — and the hidden one working in the shadows.

The First Pattern

The Scattered Creative

She starts everything with passion, ideas crowding her mind, until the loveliest ones drift away unfinished.

Why do you begin with such fire, yet nothing reaches its end?
The Second Pattern

Perfection's Captive

She revises, refines, and postpones the unveiling — until her best work stays locked away, unseen.

Why do you keep polishing until the work stays safe from every eye?
The Third Pattern

The Clever Avoider

She finishes every small thing with skill, while circling the one thing that matters — never stepping inside.

Why do you postpone the single thing that would change your life?
The Fourth Pattern

The Depleted Capable

Everyone loads her up because she "can handle it," while layers of silent exhaustion gather inside.

Why do you carry everything, as if your standing would fall the moment you stopped?
