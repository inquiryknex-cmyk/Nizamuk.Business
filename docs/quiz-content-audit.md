# Quiz content audit — the 12 scenes × 48 answers

**Purpose.** Companion to the v3 mechanic change (closest +2 / optional second
+1 / no «الأبعد»). If a visitor regularly feels *three* answers fit equally,
the answers themselves are under-differentiated — so each scene was audited
for mechanism overlap before traffic is driven to the quiz. **No answer text
was changed in this pass**; this is the evidence base for a copy-sharpening
pass the owner approves separately.

**The test applied to every option:** does it describe the pattern's
*distinguishing mechanism* (why she stalls) rather than a *surface behavior*
(that she stalls)? Surface behaviors overlap; mechanisms don't.

## Summary

- **8 / 12 scenes are sharp or excellent.** No scene fails.
- **The one systematic adjacency is Mutafadiya ↔ Kafua** ("I postponed it" vs.
  "it got displaced by others") in scenes 3 and 5. The new optional-second
  mechanic actually *absorbs* this gracefully — a woman who feels both no
  longer has to falsify one; she marks the truer one closest and the other
  as «قريبة أيضًا», and the scoring keeps both signals.
- **Framing fix shipped with v3:** every scene now carries the instruction
  «اختاري الأقرب إليكِ — ما يتكرر معكِ غالبًا» under the question, and the
  intro card teaches: «قد تجدين نفسكِ في أكثر من إجابة — وهذا طبيعي. اختاري
  الأقرب إليكِ: ما يتكرر معكِ غالبًا، لا ما حدث مرة أو مرتين.»
- **Resolved (owner-approved 2026-07-16):** the two Kafua options were
  sharpened toward *who takes the time* rather than *that time was lost*:
  - Scene 3: «كل ما جئتُ أفتحها سبقني طلبُ أحدٍ اعتاد أني «ما أقصّر»؛ وقتي
    يُصرف على الجميع أولًا — ودورتي تنتظر دوري أنا.»
  - Scene 5 (owner's wording): «لا يسألني أحد إن كنتُ متفرغة؛ لازم أستلم
    الموسم كله، ومشروعي أول ما يُهمَّش.»
  Both lead with the mechanism — she is claimed, not asked — closing the
  Mutafadiya ↔ Kafua adjacency flagged above. No slug, scoring, or structural
  change.
